About KTM

KTM is one of the most popular motorcycle brands known for performance and racing spirit.

Founded in Austria, KTM has expanded globally with a strong presence in India. The brand is recognized for its aggressive design, cutting-edge technology, and thrilling ride experience. From the urban streets with Duke series to adventurous trails with Adventure series, KTM continues to inspire riders.
